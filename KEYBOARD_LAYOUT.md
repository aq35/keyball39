# Keyball39 Keyboard Layout

This document provides an overview of the keyboard layout for Keyball39, a custom keyboard tailored for ergonomic design and productivity.

## Layer 0: Base
The base layer contains the primary keys used for most typing tasks.

| Row \ Col | 1  | 2  | 3  | 4  | 5 | 6 (Space Center) | 7  | 8  | 9  | 10 |

| **Row 1** | Q   | W   | E   | R   | T  |                | Y   | U   | I   | O   |
| **Row 2** | A   | S   | D   | F   | G  |                | H   | J   | K   | L   |
| **Row 3** | Z   | X   | C   | V   | B  |                | N   | M   | ,   | .   |

## Custom Key Combinations
- **H + J**: Enter Key (prevents accidental presses)
- **Q + W**: Activates Layer 2 while held down

Further details are available in the [keymap.c file](./keymap.c).
